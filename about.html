About

LocalHelps was created to help make donating easy. When doing spring cleaning we may often find ourselves with an assortment of used items. Finding charities that match your specific needs can be a hassle, and that is where LocalHelps come in! Simply enter your location, and categories of items you want to donate to receive a list of eligible charities.

Currently LocalHelps only operates in Metro Vancouver, however our system is built to support other citites throught he use of crowdsourcing suggestions.

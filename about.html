About

LocalHelps was created to help make donating easy. Whether you're moving, spring-cleaning, or just trying to de-clutter your space, you're bound to find some old items with lots of life left in them. Finding charities that match your specific needs can be a hassle, and that is where LocalHelps comes in! Simply enter your location, and select the type of item you want to donate to receive a list of eligible charities.

CLocalHelps currently only operates in Metro Vancouver, however our system is built to support other cities through the use of crowd-sourcing suggestions. Help charitable givers in your area find organizations to donate their pre-loved items to by suggesting a charity near you!
